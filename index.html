About

Magenta Diamond's Origin is a fan-made comic series (Diego Souza), based on the Steven Universe universe by Rebecca Sugar. This story is set in an alternate universe and has been developed with great care for over 5 years.

An original project that combines mystery, drama, fantasy and science fiction, addressing themes such as maturation, identity, friendships and discoveries. The visual identity and narrative reflect the unique personality of the story.

Who is Magenta Diamond? Magenta Diamond is one of the 15 Diamonds on the planet Color Planet, living in the midst of a rigid and conservative empire. Despite the pressures of her world, she stands out for her curious, outgoing and empathetic personality — although, at times, she shows a certain laziness and a touch of drama. Her journey is marked by ups and downs: between discoveries, traumas and intense moments, Magenta seeks to understand her true place among the other Diamonds. In her free time, she expresses herself by drawing, exploring new places and planning creative plans with her closest friends.
