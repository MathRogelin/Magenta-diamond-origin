About

"Magenta Diamond's Origin" is a fan-made comic series inspired by the Steven Universe universe, created by Rebecca Sugar. The story takes place in an alternate universe (AU), developed with dedication and care over more than five years, exploring new perspectives within this beloved world.

This is an independent, authorial project that combines mystery, drama, fantasy, and science fiction, addressing themes such as growing up, identity, friendship, loss, and self-discovery. The work seeks to convey deep emotions and reflections through an engaging narrative and a unique visual identity, created specifically to represent the essence of the story and its characters.

"Magenta Diamond's Origin" is a non-profit project, made by fans for fans, with no official affiliation with the original series. All content is produced with respect, admiration, and love for the Steven Universe universe, celebrating its message of empathy, acceptance, and diversity.

History

A thousand years ago, on Color Planet, Magenta Diamond was born — a young Diamond unlike any other. Raised under strict imperial rules and traditions of perfection, she always felt different. She questions power, hierarchy, and what it truly means to be a Diamond.

While trying to fulfill her role, Magenta uncovers hidden talents and begins to see the empire’s cracks. Her curiosity exposes secrets that challenge everything she once believed. Through friendships, loss, and inner conflict, she begins a journey of self-discovery and change, facing the cost of defying an ancient system.

Each chapter marks her evolution — from a confused, curious young gem into a Diamond who redefines the meaning of power and identity.
